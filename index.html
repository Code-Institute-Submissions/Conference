About

Stockholm Youth Conference is a conference that happens annually in June in Friends Arena stadium in Stockholm Sweden. The purpose of this conference is to educate the youth about self awareness, purpose, value and much more. We have the best and experienced motivational speakers from all over the world who teaches this young adults based on their experiences. Youth from all over the world aged between 18 to 25 gathers to attend. This conference is free but one have to search,book and pays for their accomodation, food, transport and everything they need while attending the conference.
